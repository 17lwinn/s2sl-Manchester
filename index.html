To donate to keep our hospitals running press the button below. Your money will be donated to the Manchester University NHS Foundation Trust Charity

Why?

During these unprecedented times it is more important than ever to have an active health service, so that we can stay safe and live our lives like usual. The NHS are doing their best providing us with the health care we need, for everyone. By donating, volunteering or doing something else you are supporting the very people keeping our city and our country safe and turning.

I don't have any money, what else can I do to help?

There are many other things you can do to help, like these:

  • Volunteer for the NHS (paused for now)
  • Stay home and don't visit hospitals, pharmacies, GPs, etc. unless necessary
  • Follow the government and NHS guidlines
  • Use your computer's power to find a cure or vaccine with Folding@home
  • Script HTML, CSS, etc. to save lives with Scripting To Save Lives
